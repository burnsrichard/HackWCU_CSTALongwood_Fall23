CSTA Longwood Hackathon / HackWCU

An in-person and virtual hackathon for local high school and college level students. Join us and local students and tech professionals a day of innovation, fun, and creative problem solving. We'll have free food, activities, and prizes while you build exciting projects.

Sunday, September 24, 10a - 4p

West Chester University


25 University Ave. (UNA), Rm 158 Campus Map

Call for Participation

The local chapter of CSTA Longwood is excited to partner with the Department of Computer Science at West Chester University to host an in-person and virtual hackathon for local high school and college students in our surrounding Berks, Chester, Lancaster, and York counties. (Other counties and out-of-state are welcome to join too!)

Registration

The cost for attending is $0, made possible by the support of our sponsors.

The hackathon will be held in 25 University Ave, Rm 158, which houses the Computer Science Department. Please enter through the main doors. Free parking is at the Sharpless Parking Garage.
