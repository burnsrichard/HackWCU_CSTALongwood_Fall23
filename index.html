CSTA Longwood Hackathon

An in-person and virtual hackathon for local high school and college level students.

Sunday, September 24, 10a - 4p

West Chester University

Call for Participation

The local chapter of CSTA Longwood is excited to partner with the Department of Computer Science at West Chester University to host an in-person and virtual hackthon for local high school and college students in our surrounding Berks, Chester, Lancaster, and York counties. (Other counties and out-of-state are welcome to join too!)

Registration

The cost for attending is $0, made possible by the support of our sponsors.

A registration link for attendees will be posted.

Important Dates

  • Friday, September 8: Sponsortship Deadline
  • Sunday, September 17: Attendee Registration Deadline
  • Sunday, September 24: CSTA Longwood & HackWCU
